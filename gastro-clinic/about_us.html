ABOUT US

Gastro Clinics offers a broad spectrum of diagnosis, preventive treatments and professional medical care to individuals and patients, inclusive of specialised medical segments of General Medicine, General Gastro and advanced therapeutic endoscopic procedures. Our clinic specialises in the diagnosis and treatment of acute conditions that leads to the development of chronic diseases and conditions concerning the human digestive system.

The faculty members have a major clinical, advance research interest and a long-term applied medical experience in Gastroenterology, Endoscopy (endoscopes in upper GI), Weight Loss Endoscopy Procedures & Gastric Balloon Procedures, Hepatology, Sigmoidoscopy and Colonoscopy procedures. Additionally, high resolution of ERCP for gall stone removals, cancer treatment, IBS, IBD, Liver, abdominal pain, diarrhea, constipation, coeliac, reflux, and gastritis is carried out by expert hands.

Our chief medical consultant and specialist has attained training in Gastroenterology within the NHS for over 15 years. Hence, with pragmatic long-term experience, cutting-edge knowledge in the relevant fields and by incorporating the use of state-of-the-art technology, our clinic serves every patient with exclusive medical care only. Nevertheless, we possess some of the most advanced testing and treatment facilities, which gives our patients the benefit of faster on-site diagnosis and comprehensive and personalised care under expert supervision.

Gastro Clinics takes humble pride to quote that our specialists are expertly equipped to deliver high quality care to the patients with the help of rock solid background in the medical industry. With over 15 years of certified experience, specialised training in the fields of gastroenterology and hepatology, our medical specialist is recognised as an authentic and competent practitioner in multiple and diverse areas of gastroenterology.

Gastroenterology is concerned with the diagnosis and treatment of conditions affecting the stomach and intestines, by studying the normal function and syndromes of the esophagus, stomach, small intestine, colon and rectum, pancreas, gallbladder, bile ducts and liver. In regard, backed up by a vast training skills and experience, our practitioner owns a detailed understanding and recognition of the (physiology) of the gastrointestinal organs. This includes the movement, digestion, absorption and removal of food and nutrients from the body, along with the function and role the liver plays as a digestive organ. Hence, all common as well as chronic conditions are examined, analysed and treated under expert administration of Gastro Clinics.

Whether it is a routine test or involves a complex disease treatment, our multidisciplinary team of consultants work together to provide our patients with the best personalised care that is required. Thus, Gastro Clinics examines and treats a broad range of conditions and symptoms and carries out extensive procedures that includes;

  • Gastrointestinal systems – such as abdominal pain, altered bowel movements and difficulty swallowing – and their liver (or hepatic) system – such as jaundice, pale coloured stools and enlarged livers.
  • Hepatology and Endoscopy (independent endoscopes in upper GI).
  • Flexible Sigmoidoscopy and Colonoscopy procedures – diagnosing and treating conditions via gastroscopy, colonoscopy, complex polypectomy.
  • Advanced therapeutic procedures like endoscopic fistula closures, dilatations, stenting and ERCPs.
  • Endoscopic ultrasonography.
  • Weight loss endoscopic procedures & Gastric balloon procedures.
  • ERCP for gall stone removals, cancer treatment, IBS, IBD, liver, abdominal pain, diarrhea, constipation, coeliac, reflux and gastritis.
  • PEG insertions & management of active GI bleeding independently.

Having been training in Gastroenterology within the NHS for over 15 years, I have been a consultant for over 5 years and currently work as a consultant gastroenterologist at Barking Havering and Redbridge University Hospitals NHS Trust. There, I regularly see a variety of patients with all sorts of problems to do with their gastrointestinal systems – such as abdominal pain, altered bowel movements and difficulty swallowing – and their liver (or hepatic) system – such as jaundice, pale coloured stools and enlarged livers. I also look after patients who have emergency bleeding into their guts. I also perform a variety of treatments using endoscopy (a telescope with instruments that is either inserted through the mouth into the upper part of the gut or through the anus into the lower part of the gut). My training involved a specialist training programme in gastroenterology and hepatology, in which I learnt and was assessed as being competent in terms of my knowledge and practical skills in a variety of areas related to gastroenterology. Furthermore, I have been formally assessed by the JAG (Joint Advisory Group of GI Endoscope) and deemed competent for independent endoscopes in upper GI, Flexible Sigmoidoscopy and Colonoscopic procedures. That means that, if you need it, I can help diagnose and treat your problems by performing a gastroscopy, colonoscopy, complex polypectomy, advanced therapeutic procedures like endoscopic fistula closures, stenting and ERCPs. I also perform endoscopic ultrasound examinations having completed my advanced training in endoscopic ultrasonography in the prestigious unit at Montreal University Hospital under the tutelage of Professor Anand Sahai. Having attended more than 20 national and international conferences and presented over 10 times, with 5 publications in high impact journals, I always seek to stay at the cutting edge of gastroenterology, so that I can give my patients the best, evidenced-based treatment options to help them to recover. I have also taught Cambridge University and Oxford University medical students as well as being a course examiner for the Royal College of Physicians.
