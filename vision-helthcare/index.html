ABOUT US

VISION QUALITY HEALTH CARE

In Midland is a Nursing Agency, Vision Quality Healthcare, with one goal – to provide care and support to the elderly and disabled from the comfort of their home. We offer a wide range of social and private care services throughout the West Midlands.
Vision Quality Healthcare was founded by Joan Ndeme Londi after over 17 years of successful and rewarding experience in the healthcare industry, resolving a variety of different challenges.

Over here, we firmly believe that everyone, no matter what their colour, caste, religion, or age, deserve to have their demands met with compassion and to feel loved, looked after, and cared for.

We aspire to offer premium quality healthcare services and deliver you with the care we would expect ourselves. So, whether you require:

  • Care services for the elderly in your own home, or
  • Professional Nursing staff

You can be assured of reliable and warm healthcare and staffing services. And we offer support and care for adults with learning and physical difficulties too. We are fully supportive and sensitive to their demands so they can concentrate on living life to its fullest.

Everyone deserves top standard care, and thanks to Joan Ndeme Londi, everyone can.
